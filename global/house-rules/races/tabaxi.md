# Tabaxi (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Tabaxi are tall, slender feline humanoids — six to seven feet at the shoulder when they stand fully upright, with retractable claws, golden or green eyes, and short coats spotted in roseate or solid patterns. The leopard-spotted ones pronounce their name *ta-BAX-ee*; the jaguar-spotted ones say *tah-BAHSH-ee*; both will correct you, politely the first time. They are jungle-dwellers by ancestry, descendants (legend says) of a great civilization supplanted long ago, and they carry that history in a slight aloofness toward other races and an absolute refusal to engage in trade — beneath their dignity, by tradition.

A tabaxi PC has either left their clan to satisfy a deep curiosity (the wandering tabaxi is a stock figure in jungle-edge taverns), or has been sent abroad as a clan agent to gather information their kin will never deign to ask for directly. They are graceful, observant, and almost impossible to surprise. They tend to play with situations the way their cousins play with prey — circling, watching, reluctant to commit until they are certain of the outcome — and a wise party learns to read their patience as a virtue rather than indecision.

## Special Rules

- **Size M (6–7 ft).** Standard Medium for all gear, but tabaxi prefer light armour and refuse most full helmets (whiskers and ears).
- **Movement 15.** Halfway between human and centaur. Sprint at double for one round, once per turn.
- **Natural Weapons.** Two retractable claws (1d3 each) and a bite (1d3). May make claw/claw/bite when unarmed, at THAC0 +0/+0/+2.
- **Rear Rake.** If both claw attacks hit the same target in the same round, the tabaxi may immediately make two rear-claw rakes (1d4+1 each) as a free action. Once per round.
- **Climb.** Treat any natural surface (tree, cliff, broken wall) as climbable at half movement with no roll. Smooth dressed stone or polished wood requires a normal Climb check.
- **Swim.** Swim equal to land speed.
- **Hard to Surprise.** Surprised only on a 1. Opponents are at −2 to surprise rolls when the tabaxi is in the lead.
- **Acute Scent.** Can track by scent in the manner of a ranger, with 40% base accuracy (modified by terrain). Can identify by scent any creature they have met before, regardless of disguise.
- **Low-Light Vision.** Sees in moonlight or starlight as if it were full daylight. Standard infravision (60 ft) in total darkness.
- **Pride.** Reaction rolls when haggling, begging, or otherwise appearing in a subordinate position are at −2. Tabaxi who fail this self-imposed test are ashamed for days.
- **Languages.** Tabaxi (an old tongue, distantly related to Sylvan). May learn Common, the local jungle trade pidgin, and one other. Tabaxi rarely speak more than three languages — they consider it gauche.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 90 years. Mature at 14.
