# Faerie Dragon (Homebrew PC Race)

*Based on the `dragonet, faerie dragon` monster entry — a tiny, butterfly-winged dragon of genius intellect.*

## Description

Faerie dragons are the smallest and strangest of true dragons: a foot of iridescent, cat-bodied dragon trailing a long prehensile tail, borne aloft on gossamer butterfly wings that shimmer through every colour of their mood. Their scales shift hue with age, from red through the spectrum to a final silver-violet, and behind their bright eyes sits a genius wit bent almost entirely toward mischief. They infest pleasant woodlands in loose, gossipy clans, playing elaborate pranks on anything that wanders past and remembering every insult and kindness for a century.

A faerie dragon PC is a featherweight genius: physically the frailest creature most parties will ever field, and one of the cleverest. It cannot fight, lift, or carry to any meaningful degree. Instead it flits about the battlefield unseen, breathes giddy confusion into a knot of enemies, and turns a sharp, magical mind to problems that muscle cannot solve. Travelling companions either learn to love the constant pranks or learn to sleep with one eye open.

## Special Rules

- **Size T (1 ft, ~3 lb).** Cannot wear armour or wield any weapon heavier than a dagger (used as a spear) — and realistically fights with magic and breath, not steel. Tiny, erratic flight grants **+2 AC vs. Medium or larger attackers**. Negligible carrying capacity.
- **Manipulation (the "hands" question).** Like the pseudodragon, the faerie dragon has clawed forelimbs and a **prehensile tail**, so it can perform somatic spell components, manage a spellbook and material components, pick a lock, and handle small tools — it is a caster and a trickster, not a helpless animal.
- **Frail.** Roll Hit Dice at **−2 per die (minimum 1 per die)**. A faerie dragon has shockingly few hit points; it survives by never being where the blow lands.
- **Flight.** Move 6 on the ground, **Fly 24 (Class A — perfect manoeuvrability)**: it can hover, turn on a pinpoint, and fly in any enclosed space.
- **Invisibility (signature).** Turns **invisible at will**, as the spell, and — uniquely — may remain invisible while moving and observing. It becomes visible for **1 round** after it makes an attack or casts an offensive spell, then may vanish again. While invisible its AC improves by 4.
- **Euphoria Breath.** Once every 3 rounds, breathes a 2-ft cloud of glittering gas. Each creature in the cloud saves vs. breath weapon or is overcome with giddy **euphoria for 1d6 rounds** (acts as though *confused*, harmless and grinning — drops weapons, wanders, giggles). Harmless but devastating to a coordinated enemy line.
- **Innate Cantrips.** Even without the Mage class, every faerie dragon knows a handful of minor innate spells, usable at will: *dancing lights*, *ventriloquism*, *audible glamer*, and *mending* — the toolkit of a born prankster. A Mage-classed faerie dragon casts its class spells normally on top of these.
- **Telepathy.** Communicates by telepathy with any creature within 60 ft, and may speak normally as well (faerie dragons are chatterboxes). Genius intellect; near-perfect memory.
- **Infravision 60 ft.**
- **Languages.** Draconic, Sylvan, Common, plus the ability to *speak with animals* at will. Learns further languages with ease.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 300 years. Matures at 5; scale colour advances with age.
