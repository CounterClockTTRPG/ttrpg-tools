# Doppelganger (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

In their natural form, doppelgangers stand roughly human-sized, with grey, hairless skin stretched over an oddly proportioned skeleton, large white eyes, and slack features that never quite settle. They almost never appear in that form. From childhood a doppelganger learns to wear other faces, and most go their entire lives without showing the one beneath.

The race is deeply distrusted, and with reason: the bulk of doppelgangers earn their living by infiltration, replacement, and quiet murder. A doppelganger PC has rejected that path, or never been taught it, but is unlikely to convince anyone of that fact. Most travel under stable assumed identities, change them rarely, and tell only their closest companions the truth — if anyone at all. They tend to be cautious, observant, and uncomfortably good at picking up the speech patterns and small habits of the people around them.

Mechanically, a doppelganger PC is balanced by sharply limited level progression and the social cost of their species. Played well, they are not assassins; they are people whose first instinct, in any new situation, is to study and adapt.

## Special Rules

- **Size M.** True form is roughly 5½ ft and 150 lb. Assumed forms must fall within ±1 ft of height and ±50% of mass.
- **Shapechange.** At will, as a full round of concentration, the doppelganger may assume the form of any humanoid (Small to Large) they have observed for at least one full minute. The change is physical, not illusory: it survives *detect magic*, *true seeing* (which reveals the true form), and physical inspection. Voice, mannerisms, and clothing are mimicked at the player's discretion; mechanical disguise checks (or NPC Spot/Insight rolls) still apply for fine detail. Equipment carried does **not** transform — only the doppelganger's body and the clothing worn at the moment of change.
- **Read Surface Thoughts.** May read the surface thoughts of any creature within 30 ft, concentrating one round (save vs. spell negates, target unaware unless they make the save by 5+). Three times per day. This is how full doppelgangers maintain disguises; a PC must use it consciously and pays for it.
- **Save Bonuses.** +4 to saves vs. *sleep*, *charm*, *hold*, and any mind-affecting spell. Innate to the species and not lost in any form.
- **Tough Hide.** Natural AC 5 in true form (before Dex). In assumed forms the doppelganger uses normal AC for that body, plus armour worn.
- **Slow to Mature.** Doppelgangers reach adulthood at 50 and may live 300+ years. Starting age is 50–80.
- **Distrust.** Once a community knows what the PC is, reaction rolls in that community are at −4 permanently. The PC is generally treated as a known criminal whether or not they have committed any crime.
- **Languages.** Common, plus 1d4+1 additional languages chosen at character creation, reflecting a youth spent listening. May not begin play knowing Doppelganger unless raised by their own kind, in which case they probably know fewer of the others.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 350 years. Mature at 50.
