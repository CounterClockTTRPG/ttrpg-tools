# Centaur (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Centaurs are the woodland riders of legend: human from the waist up, heavy draught-horse below. They stand eight to nine feet tall at the head, weigh upwards of 1,500 pounds, and can outpace a galloping horse on rough ground. Most centaur tribes shun humans, share their forests with elves on tolerant terms, and live by hunting, foraging, and small-scale horticulture. They mate for life, raise their young in common, and are deeply, almost religiously protective of their woodlands and their families.

A centaur PC is usually an exile, a scout, or the rare individual whose curiosity outweighed their tribe's distrust of outsiders. They struggle with the obvious problems — doorways, ship cabins, second storeys, most dungeons — but in open country, mounted opponents quickly learn that a centaur is faster, hits harder, and never tires. They have a reputation for becoming loud and aggressive when drunk, which they will admit, with a slight smile, is mostly true.

## Special Rules

- **Size L (8–9 ft tall, ~1,500 lb).** Cannot enter any space narrower than 5 ft or shorter than 8 ft without squeezing (half move, −2 to attacks). Stairs designed for humans are difficult terrain. Cannot climb ladders. Cannot ride in normal carts, boats under 20 ft, or sit in human furniture.
- **Movement 18.** Roughly 1.5× a human's running speed. Never tires before a horse would.
- **Three Attacks.** Each round in melee: one weapon attack (at normal THAC0) and two hoof attacks (1d6 each, at THAC0+2). The hoof attacks may also be made against a target the centaur has just trampled past on a charge.
- **Trample / Overrun.** May attempt to overrun any Medium or smaller creature in their path. Target saves vs. paralysis or is knocked prone and takes 1d6 damage; the centaur continues their move.
- **Lance Charge.** When wielding a lance from a charge of 30+ ft in a straight line, deal double weapon damage. The centaur is −1 AC for the round.
- **Carrying Capacity.** Treat as a light warhorse for load purposes — roughly 4× human encumbrance. May carry one Medium humanoid passenger.
- **Centaur Armour.** Standard armour costs 4× and weighs 2× — must be custom-built. Most centaurs wear partial barding (chest/shoulders only) for AC 7, or scale and shield for AC 5.
- **Woodland Stride.** No movement penalty for natural undergrowth, brambles, or shallow streams.
- **Infravision 60 ft.** (Centaurs see roughly as elves do.)
- **Languages.** Centaur, Sylvan, Common. May learn Elven (10% start with it).
- **Lifespan.** Up to 120 years. Mature at 18.
