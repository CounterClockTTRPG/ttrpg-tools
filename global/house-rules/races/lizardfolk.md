# Lizardfolk (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Lizardfolk are tall, semi-aquatic reptilian humanoids — six to seven feet of muscle, scale, and tooth, with a heavy three-foot tail used for balance rather than gripping. Their scales run from dark green to mottled brown to swamp-grey. They are an old race, older than humans in most reckonings, and most tribes have never had any reason to evolve past raiding, fishing, and the occasional grim feast on a captured prisoner.

A lizardfolk PC is usually one of the rare hatchlings born clever enough to find their tribe's habits unsettling, or one of the (also rare) more advanced tribes that has learned trade and tolerance. They tend to speak slowly, eat in silence, and react to bad news with an unblinking patience that other races read as either menace or wisdom depending on the situation. They make excellent guides through swamp and marsh, hold their breath longer than any humanoid alive, and never complain about the cold — though they fight badly in it.

## Special Rules

- **Size M (6–7 ft).** Tail extends another 3–4 ft behind. Counts as Medium for all carrying and weapon purposes, but cannot wear armour designed for tailless races without a tailored cutout (1.5× cost).
- **Natural Armour.** Scaled hide gives base AC 5 with no armour. Stacks with shield bonus only; wearing manufactured armour overrides the natural AC rather than adding to it. Most lizardfolk PCs wear hide or studded leather over the scales for AC 4.
- **Natural Weapons.** Two claws (1d3 each) and a bite (1d6). May make claw/claw/bite at THAC0 +0/+0/+2 in any round they have no melee weapon drawn. With a weapon drawn, they may add a single bite at the off-hand penalty.
- **Swimmer.** Swim 12 (twice land speed). Holds breath for CON × 4 rounds (typical PC: 60+ rounds, 6 minutes). Swim checks for combat manoeuvres are at +4.
- **Cold-Blooded.** In any environment colder than roughly 10°C / 50°F, the lizardfolk suffers −1 to attack and damage and Move is reduced by 3. In freezing weather, they must save vs. paralysis at the start of each day or be torpid (half actions only) until they reach warmth.
- **Hold Breath.** As above; also immune to drowning damage for the duration.
- **Tail Balance.** +2 to saves vs. being knocked prone or pushed off balance. Cannot be tripped by a single opponent.
- **Reptilian Stomach.** Can eat raw meat, carrion, and brackish water without ill effect. Save vs. poison at +2 against ingested poisons and natural disease.
- **No Infravision.** Lizardfolk see in normal light only — a frequent surprise to parties expecting otherwise.
- **Languages.** Lizardfolk (Draconic-derived), Common (slow). May learn Troglodyte, Bullywug, Sahuagin (with effort).
- **Lifespan.** Up to 80 years. Mature at 12.
