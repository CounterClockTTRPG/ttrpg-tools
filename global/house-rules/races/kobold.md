# Kobold (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Kobolds are small, scaly humanoids barely 3 feet tall, with rust-coloured hides, bright red eyes, and twin horns of tan or ivory. They smell faintly of damp dog and stagnant water — a fact most kobolds resent and most others remark on. Their reputation in the wider world is dire: cowardly, sadistic, treacherous. A kobold PC is a rare exception, an outcast who has either fled their tribe or been raised among other races, and who has learned that survival sometimes means fighting alongside the very creatures their kin would ambush in a tunnel.

What kobolds lack in size and strength they make up in cunning, patience, and an almost supernatural feel for traps, mines, and dark places. They are tribal by instinct even when alone, and a kobold adventurer often becomes fiercely loyal to whatever party will tolerate them.

## Attribute Modifiers

- Dexterity +1
- Constitution −1
- Charisma −2 (against non-kobolds)

## Special Rules

- **Size S.** Cannot wield weapons larger than Medium. Two-handed weapons are out of the question. Bonus +1 to AC against Large or larger opponents (small target).
- **Infravision 60 ft.**
- **Sunlight Sensitivity.** −1 to attack rolls in direct sunlight or equivalent magical light.
- **Trap Sense.** +10% to the thief Find Traps skill (or 1-in-6 base chance for non-thieves to spot mechanical traps in stone or earthen construction).
- **Mining Eye.** Can detect grade or slope (1-in-6), new construction (1-in-6), and approximate depth underground (1-in-4). Functions as the dwarven racial ability but only in worked tunnels, never in natural caves.
- **Pack Tactics.** When two or more allies are within 5 ft of the same target, +1 to hit.
- **Hated Enemy: Gnomes.** Reaction rolls with gnomes are at −4 in both directions; both sides know it on sight.
- **Languages.** Kobold, Common (broken). May learn Goblin, Orc, Draconic, Undercommon.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 135 years. Adulthood at 8.
