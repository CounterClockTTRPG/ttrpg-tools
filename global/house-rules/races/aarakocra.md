# Aarakocra (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Aarakocra are bird-folk of the high peaks: five feet tall standing, twenty feet across in flight. Their faces are something between parrot and eagle, their plumage bright in the males (red, orange, yellow) and muted in the females (brown, grey). Each wing carries a three-fingered hand at its halfway point, and their taloned feet unfold into a second pair of hands when they perch. Their bones are hollow — light enough for flight, fragile enough to break in a heavy fall.

Aarakocra are profoundly territorial and profoundly claustrophobic. Most live and die without ever entering a cave or building. An aarakocra PC is therefore a remarkable individual: an exile, a scout sent on a long errand, or one of the rare few who learned to swallow the panic of enclosed spaces. They tend to be polite, curious, and easily homesick. They make excellent scouts and skirmishers but poor dungeoneers, and a wise party plans accordingly.

## Special Rules

- **Flight.** Move 6 on the ground, Fly 36 (Class C — good but not perfect manoeuvrability). Cannot fly while wearing more than leather armour or carrying more than 50% of normal load. Cannot fly indoors in any space narrower than 20 ft of clear vertical room.
- **Hollow Bones.** −2 hp per Hit Die rolled (minimum 1 per HD). Falls and crushing damage are taken at +1 damage per die.
- **Diving Attack.** Requires at least 200 ft of dive distance. The aarakocra makes a single attack with a held weapon (typically javelin) at +4 to hit and double damage. May be made once per round, only on the round the dive ends.
- **Aerial Mastery.** No penalty for missile fire while flying. +1 to hit with thrown weapons in general.
- **Keen Sight.** Spot details at twice human range in daylight. No infravision.
- **Claustrophobia.** Save vs. paralysis on first entering any enclosed space (cave, building interior, tunnel) or be unable to enter for 1 turn. Once inside, save again every turn or suffer −2 to all rolls until back in the open. A trusted companion holding the aarakocra's hand grants +2 to all such saves.
- **Air-Caller.** Five aarakocra performing a 3-round aerial chant (one of them a shaman or druid) may summon a willing air elemental as the spell. The elemental does not risk its life for them. Once per week.
- **Languages.** Aarakocra, the language of giant eagles, Auran. 50% chance to know Common (with a sharp accent).
- **Lifespan.** Up to 30 years. Adulthood at 3, full size at 5.
