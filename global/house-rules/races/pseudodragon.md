# Pseudodragon (Homebrew PC Race)

*Based on the `dragonet, pseudodragon` monster entry — a cat-sized true dragon. (The party already knows one: Vex.)*

## Description

Pseudodragons are true dragons in miniature — barely a foot and a half from snout to vent, with another foot of prehensile, sting-tipped tail. Their scales are a burnished red-brown that shifts to match their surroundings, their wings beat fast and quiet, and their eyes hold an unmistakable reptilian cunning. They are mute, communicating by projecting feelings and simple images into the minds of those they trust, and they bond fiercely and selectively. Most spend their lives as the prized companions of wizards and rangers; a free-willed pseudodragon adventuring on its own account is a rare and strong-minded individual.

A pseudodragon PC trades brawn for guile, reach, and the sky. It cannot match a warrior blow for blow — it weighs less than a housecat — but it flies, it vanishes against any backdrop, its tail carries a sleep that fells creatures many times its size, and it always knows exactly how much its companions can be trusted. Parties learn to treat it not as a pet but as the small, watchful, occasionally smug person it is.

## Special Rules

- **Size T (1½ ft, ~7 lb).** Cannot wear manufactured armour or wield any weapon larger than a dagger (used two-handed, as a short spear). Being a Tiny, darting target grants **+2 AC against Medium or larger attackers**. Carrying capacity is a fraction of a human's — a pseudodragon travels light or not at all.
- **Manipulation (the "hands" question).** A pseudodragon is **not** a hands-less beast: its two clawed forelimbs and **fully prehensile tail** let it work locks, levers, pouches, and somatic gestures, and wield a dagger or light tool. Fine manual tasks take a little longer (it has small claws, not fingers), but nothing is closed to it. This is the difference between a pseudodragon and, say, a true animal.
- **Natural Armour.** Scaled hide gives base AC 3 (before Dexterity); cannot be improved by worn armour, only by Dex, magic, and cover.
- **Flight.** Move 6 on the ground, **Fly 24 (Class B — good manoeuvrability)**. Light enough to fly indoors and in tight quarters, unlike larger winged races. Cannot fly carrying more than a light load.
- **Sting (signature).** The tail-sting hits for 1d3 plus venom: target saves vs. poison or **falls asleep for 1d6 turns** (a potent soporific, not lethal). The venom can be delivered up to **three times per day** before the gland is spent.
- **Chameleon Hide.** By holding still and shifting colour, the pseudodragon **hides in natural terrain or shadow at 90%** (a thief adds this to Hide in Shadows, to a max of 99%) and surprises on a roll of 1–4 from concealment.
- **Telepathic Bond.** Communicates telepathically with any creature it can see within 60 ft (it cannot speak aloud). It also senses the general emotional state and honesty of those it converses with — pseudodragons are famously hard to lie to.
- **Sense Invisible.** Detects invisible or hidden creatures within 20 ft on a successful Wisdom check.
- **Infravision 60 ft.**
- **Languages.** Understands Common and Draconic; expresses itself only by telepathy and image-sending. May learn to understand further languages normally.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 150 years. Matures at 6.
