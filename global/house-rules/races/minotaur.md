# Minotaur (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Minotaurs stand seven and a half feet tall, with the muscular body of a man and the head of a bull — broad-horned, dark-furred, and unmistakable. The race's origin is a curse: the first minotaur was a great human fighter who wished to be as strong as a bull, and the gods obliged in the most literal way available. Most minotaurs are born to the curse, raised in labyrinths and fed on prisoners, and grow into the chaotic-evil terrors of legend.

A minotaur PC is something else entirely — a cursed human reverting toward the bull-form, a born minotaur who escaped the labyrinth, or a half-breed raised among humans who never quite learned to belong. They struggle constantly with two appetites: hunger that is never fully satisfied, and rage that flares at the smallest indignity. Those who master themselves can become powerful warriors, prized for their strength, their unerring sense of direction underground, and their willingness to walk into any tunnel, however narrow or dark.

## Special Rules

- **Size L (7½ ft).** Cannot use Small weapons effectively (−2 to attack with anything sized for a halfling or smaller). Cannot wear armour built for Medium or smaller frames; gear must be custom-fitted at 3× cost.
- **Gore.** Natural attack: 1d6 damage. Usable as a secondary attack at −2 when armed (off-hand penalty), or as the primary attack when unarmed.
- **Charge.** If the minotaur moves at least 30 ft in a straight line and ends adjacent to a target at least 6 ft tall, they may make a gore attack at +2 to hit for double damage. The minotaur is −1 AC for the round.
- **Labyrinth Sense.** Never lost in any maze, dungeon, or tunnel system, however twisted. Always knows the direction of the entrance and approximate distance travelled. Immune to *maze* and equivalent spells.
- **Keen Senses.** +2 to surprise rolls (i.e. harder to surprise). Can track by scent in the manner of a ranger, with 50% base accuracy (modified by terrain as the ranger ability).
- **Infravision 60 ft.**
- **Hunger.** Must eat roughly 2× a human's daily ration or suffer fatigue (−1 to attack and damage until fed). After three days without meat specifically, save vs. spell each morning at +0 or attack the next living creature encountered.
- **Hated Enemy: Gnolls.** Reaction at −6 in both directions. Will attack on sight unless restrained by allies or a strong reason.
- **Languages.** Minotaur, Common (halting). May learn Giant, Goblin, Gnoll (to curse it).
- **Lifespan.** Up to 200 years. Adulthood at 16.
