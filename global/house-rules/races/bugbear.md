# Bugbear (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Bugbears are the giants of goblin-kind: seven feet of corded muscle under a coat of coarse, mottled hair, with wedge-shaped ears, hooked claws, and a wet pink nose set in a flat, vaguely ursine face. For all that bulk they are unnervingly quiet, padding through brush and corridor on broad clawed feet without a sound, and the first many a victim knows of a bugbear is the hand already closing on the back of their neck. Most live as raiders and bullies, lording it over lesser goblins and eating whatever — or whoever — is to hand.

A bugbear PC is a rare creature who has left the warband behind: a runaway, a mercenary who found steadier pay among other races, or one of the few who simply tired of the killing. They remain enormous, intimidating, and instinctively predatory, and most folk react to a smiling bugbear the way they would to a smiling bear. Those who earn a place in a party, though, find the bugbear a peerless ambusher and a wall of muscle that hits like a falling tree.

## Special Rules

- **Size L (7 ft, ~350 lb).** Cannot use Small weapons effectively (−2 to attack with anything sized for a halfling or smaller). Armour must be built for a Large frame at 3× cost and 2× weight.
- **Natural Armour.** Thick, matted hide gives base AC 6 unarmored. Stacks with shield and Dex only; manufactured body armour overrides it rather than adding. Most bugbear PCs wear studded leather or hide over the pelt for AC 4–5.
- **Silent Stalker.** Despite their size, bugbears surprise opponents on a roll of **1–4** (instead of 1–2) and Move Silently as a thief of their own level (a bugbear thief adds this to their normal skill, to a maximum of 99%). The signature trait of the race — a seven-foot predator that makes no noise.
- **Mighty Blows.** +2 damage on every successful melee attack, from sheer mass and leverage (in addition to Strength).
- **Ambush Instinct.** +2 to hit on any attack against a foe who is surprised or unaware, or when attacking from behind (this stacks with the normal rear-attack bonus).
- **Infravision 60 ft.**
- **Crude Reputation.** −3 to encounter reactions from "civilised" humans, dwarves, and the like, who recognise the race on sight. Goblins, hobgoblins, and orcs instead react at +2 (deference).
- **Languages.** Goblin, Common (halting), plus the bugbear dialect. May learn Hobgoblin, Orcish, Worg.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 70 years. Matures at 12.
