# Gnoll (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Gnolls are tall, hyena-headed humanoids — seven feet or more of rangy muscle, dirty yellow-brown fur, and a sloping, dog-toothed muzzle that breaks into a manic grin at the worst moments. They are a pack people, ruled by the strongest, driven by an appetite that never quite sleeps. Most gnoll bands live as nomad raiders, following stronger creatures who promise easy meat and easier plunder, and they have earned every inch of their grim reputation.

A gnoll PC has, somehow, slipped the pack — orphaned and raised by others, cast out for weakness or for too much thought, or simply gone its own way after one raid too many. The instincts remain: the lope that eats miles, the loyalty to those who hunt beside them, the hard swallow of hunger in lean times. A gnoll who masters that hunger is a tireless, ferocious companion; one who doesn't becomes exactly what the stories warn of.

## Special Rules

- **Size L (7–7½ ft).** Uses Medium weapons normally; two-handed weapons may be wielded in one hand only by exceptional individuals (DM's call, STR 18+). Armour built for a Large frame costs 3× and weighs 2×.
- **Bite.** Natural attack for 1d4, usable as a secondary attack at the off-hand penalty when armed, or as the primary attack unarmed.
- **Pack Tactics.** +1 to hit any foe already engaged in melee by an ally. While fighting alongside two or more allies, the gnoll gains +2 to morale and to saves vs. fear — gnolls are bold in numbers and skittish alone.
- **Loping Gait.** Move 12, and tireless: +4 on Constitution checks to resist fatigue from running, forced marches, or sustained pursuit. A gnoll can run down most prey over distance.
- **Carrion Gut.** Can eat carrion, rotten meat, and foul water without ill effect; +2 to saves vs. ingested poison and natural disease.
- **Infravision 60 ft.**
- **Ravenous.** Requires roughly 1.5× a human's daily ration. While underfed, suffers −1 to attack and damage until properly fed. After three days without meat, save vs. spell each morning or fixate on hunting the next sizeable creature seen.
- **Hated Enemy: Minotaurs.** Reaction at −6 in both directions; the two races loathe each other on sight and will come to blows unless restrained.
- **Languages.** Gnoll, Common (halting), Flind. May learn Goblin, Orcish.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 40 years. Matures at 12.
