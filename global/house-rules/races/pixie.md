# Pixie (Homebrew PC Race)

## Description

Pixies stand two and a half feet tall, with elven features stretched into something sharper, longer-eared, and more mischievous. Their wings are silver and moth-like; their clothing is bright, often crowned with a pointed cap and curl-toed shoes. They live three centuries on average and spend most of those years inventing pranks they will never tire of repeating.

Pixies are exceptionally intelligent — their stat block lists them as 15-16 INT — but their priorities are not those of bigger folk. They are pranksters before they are warriors, gift-givers before they are hoarders, and spectators before they are heroes. A pixie PC has, for some reason, decided to actually do something rather than watch. Perhaps a treasured glade was destroyed. Perhaps a friend was kidnapped. Perhaps the prank simply got out of hand. In any case, they remain pixies: small, invisible at will, magically gifted, and difficult to take seriously until the moment they vanish and arrows start landing in unexpected places.

## Special Rules

- **Size T (2½ ft).** Cannot wield any weapon larger than a dagger sized for halflings. Pixie bows have half the range and inflict 1d3 damage; pixie swords inflict damage as a Medium dagger (1d4). Bonus +2 to AC against Medium or larger opponents.
- **Flight.** Move 6 on the ground, Fly 12 (Class B — excellent manoeuvrability). May fly while carrying up to their own body weight (≈30 lb) and no more.
- **Natural Invisibility.** Permanently invisible at will, including all worn and carried gear (up to load limit). Becomes visible the round of any attack or overt spellcasting; may resume invisibility on the following round as a free action. While invisible, attackers suffer −4 to hit and the pixie attacks at +2.
- **Magic Resistance 25%.** Applied before any save.
- **Innate Spell-Likes.** Each usable once per day, cast at 8th level: *polymorph self*, *know alignment*, *dispel magic*, *dancing lights*, *ESP*. Additionally usable once per day: *invisibility (ally)*, *cause confusion* (touch, save vs. spell or confused until *remove curse*).
- **Sleep Arrows.** Pixie war arrows (1d4+1 damage) and special sleep arrows (no damage; target saves vs. poison or sleeps 1d6 hours, no level limit). Sleep ointment is brewed only by full pixie communities; a PC starts with 1d6 doses and must return to a faerie ring to replenish.
- **Iron Vulnerability.** Cold-wrought iron weapons inflict +1 damage per die against pixies, and the pixie's magic resistance does not apply to iron-bound magical effects.
- **Languages.** Sylvan, Pixie, Sprite, Common, Elven.
- **Lifespan.** Up to 300 years. Adulthood at 20.
